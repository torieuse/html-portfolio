The Best Movies of All Time According to Torieuse

Top 3 Movies

Betty Blue

Gorgeously shot. Each image is a work of art in this 3-hours long masterpiece.

My neighbour Totoro

A fascinating kid's anime that is both moving and funny and beautifully drawn, with a touch of mystery.

Wallace and Gromit - The Wrong Trousers

A short anime that is a mastery of storytelling, with a beautiful aesthetic and lots of humour.
